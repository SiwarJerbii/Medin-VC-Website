Investment Stages

Early
to
Growth

Pre-Seed €100K – €500K
First Believers

We write the first institutional check for scientists and engineers with a breakthrough insight. At this stage, we back the person and the science, before the market exists.

Seed €500K – €3M
Building the Core

Early product-market signal, initial team formation. We actively support recruitment, go-to-market strategy, and introductions to strategic partners across our network.

Series A €3M – €15M
Finding the Formula

Repeatable revenue, clear unit economics. We help companies define category leadership and prepare for global expansion from their home markets.

Growth €15M+
Scaling the Vision

Proven product, expanding markets. We provide follow-on capital and bridge portfolio companies to leading international co-investors for Series B and beyond.
